Our Mission

At HandiMapped, our mission is to make the University of Pittsburgh’s campus more accessible for everyone. We believe that accessibility is a right, not a privilege, and we created this platform to help students, faculty, and visitors share and find accessibility information about Pitt buildings. It is inspired by the iconic Rate My Professor app that all pitt students love and use - but just make it buildings!
