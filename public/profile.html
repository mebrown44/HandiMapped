Cathedral of Learning

Top reviews say that Cathy has bad wifi, no elevator access to the second floor, wood seating that makes it hard for elders to sit comfortably, and sharp corners on ramps that make wheelchair access difficult. As seen in the pictures

Information Science

The Information Science building has a wheel chair access ramp, but it is very narrow and has a total of 3 sharp, uphill corners, making it really hard for wheelchair users. The construction on the way to the building makes it difficult for people to access the information science building without going out of the way.
